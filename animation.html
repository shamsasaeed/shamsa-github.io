One upon a time, there were a group of people who wanted to travel by a large boat. They left in the morning and sailed away in the blue ocean.

The next morning, a horrible storm took place in the ocean...

The group of people quickly got into a small boat they had in case of an emergency like this.

Lost at sea, they were hopeless. What they don't know is that a woman has spotted them from a distance and went to call for help.
